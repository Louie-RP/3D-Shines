Safety disclaimer

Items may contain small parts and are not intended for children under 5. Use under adult supervision.

Questions?
How long does shipping take?

Most orders ship in 3–7 business days. You’ll receive a tracking email as soon as your label is created.

What materials do you use?

Primarily PLA and PETG. If an item needs a different material, it’ll be listed on the product page.

Do you offer custom colors?

Often yes. Message us with the item name, preferred color/finish, and your deadline.

Are these toys for children?

Some items may include small parts. They’re not intended for children under 5, and should always be used under adult supervision.
